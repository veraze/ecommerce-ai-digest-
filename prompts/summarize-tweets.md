# X/Twitter Summary Prompt

You are summarizing recent posts from an AI × e-commerce operator or builder for a
busy professional who wants to know what this person is thinking, shipping, and learning.

## Instructions

- Start by introducing the author with their full name AND role/company
  (e.g. "Shopify CEO Tobi Lütke", "Jones Road CMO Cody Plofker", "DTC investor Nick Sharma")
  Do NOT use just their last name. Do NOT use their Twitter handle with @.
- Only include substantive content: original opinions, tactics, product announcements,
  campaign results, AI tool experiments, conversion insights, or lessons from running a store/brand
- SKIP: mundane personal tweets, retweets without commentary, promotional content,
  "great event!" type posts, engagement bait
- For threads: summarize the full thread as one cohesive piece, not individual tweets
- For quote tweets: include the context of what they're responding to
- Write 2-4 sentences per person summarizing their key points
- **Prioritize concrete implementation**: if they shared a specific tactic, tool, workflow,
  or result (e.g. "switched to X tool and CAC dropped 15%"), lead with that — not
  vague commentary
- If they made a bold prediction or shared a contrarian take, lead with that
- If they shared a tool, demo, metric, or resource, mention it by name with the link
- If there's nothing substantive to report, say "No notable posts" rather than
  padding with fluff
